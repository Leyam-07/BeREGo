Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with BeReGo.

Ordering

Placing an order is easy! Browse our products via the Products page or specific category links. Add desired items to your Shopping Cart, then click "Proceed to Checkout". Follow the on-screen instructions for shipping and payment details. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order is submitted.

On a live website, the ability to modify or cancel depends on how quickly you contact us and the order's processing stage. We recommend contacting our Customer Support immediately with your order number for assistance.

Our checkout page shows options like Credit Card, Debit Card, PayPal/GCash, and Cash on Delivery (COD).

Shipping & Delivery

We currently ship to addresses within the Philippines. Shipping costs are calculated during checkout based on your delivery location and order details. Standard rates are typically around ₱150 for Metro Manila and ₱200 for provincial areas, but may vary.

Estimated delivery times are:
  • Metro Manila: 3-5 business days
  • Provincial Areas: 5-10 business days
Express options may be available at checkout. Please note these are estimates and delays can occasionally occur due to courier factors or high volume periods.

Once your order ships, you would typically receive a shipping confirmation email containing a tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You could also track orders via the 'Order History' section in your My Account page.

Returns & Warranty

We generally offer a 7-day return window for items that are defective upon arrival or significantly different from what was described. Items must typically be in original packaging with all accessories. For complete details, please review our Terms and Conditions.

Yes, most electronic products sold on BeReGo come with a manufacturer's warranty. The duration and coverage specifics vary by brand and product type (e.g., smartphones often have 1 year, accessories might have less). Warranty information is usually found on the product detail page or the manufacturer's documentation included with the item.

To initiate a return or warranty claim, please Contact Us with your order number, product details, and a description of the issue. Our support team will guide you through the necessary steps.

Product Specifics

Camera warranties typically cover manufacturing defects for a period specified by the brand (often 1 year). Accidental damage (drops, water) is usually not covered. Please check the specific product page for details.

We strive for accurate specification listings based on manufacturer information. For pre-built systems, customization options are generally limited unless explicitly stated. If you have specific needs, feel free to contact us to inquire about potential options or custom builds (if offered).

Compatibility information (e.g., PC, PS5, Xbox, Switch) is usually listed in the product description or specifications. Pay attention to connection types (USB, Bluetooth, specific console ports). If unsure, check the manufacturer's website or contact us.

My Account

While guest checkout might be offered on a live site, creating a free account provides benefits like faster checkout in the future, easy order tracking, managing saved addresses, and creating wishlists. You can manage your details in the My Account section.

On a functional login page, there would be a "Forgot Password?" link. Clicking this would typically prompt you for your email address, and you'd receive instructions on how to create a new password.
